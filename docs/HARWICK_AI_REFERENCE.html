The Problem

Every unanswered lead is lost revenue

Real estate teams juggle multiple channels but lack automation. Leads sit in shared inboxes, qualification takes forever, assignments are guesses, and sync to Follow Up Boss happens manually or never. By the time someone qualifies the lead, the buyer has already called a competing agent.
The industry standard is chaos: no unified inbox, no automatic qualification, no intelligent routing, and no audit trail. Teams answer with generic replies, lose context between channels, and treat each lead as a support ticket instead of a sales opportunity.
The Solution

Automation that knows when to pause

Harwick AI works like a highly trained ISA: it qualifies leads in real time, suggests next actions, routes to the right agent, and keeps everything in sync. When confidence is high and context is clear, the system acts. When doubt emerges—or when a human needs control—the system pauses and surfaces the decision to an operator.
The result is faster first response, better lead quality at assignment, clear audit trails, and agents who stay in control. No surprises. No lost context.
The Workflow

From lead arrival to agent assignment

Phase 1
Lead Arrives
A prospect texts an Instagram DM, comments on a listing post, or calls a routed phone number. Harwick captures the channel, sender info, and initial message.
instant capture
Phase 2
AI Responds Naturally
AI generates a human-sounding reply, asking clarifying questions or offering next steps. If confidence is high, the reply is auto-sent. If not, it appears in the operator queue for review.
context-aware
Phase 3
AI Qualifies
Harwick asks one question at a time: intent, timeline, price range, area, property type, financing status. Each answer refines the lead score and routing logic.
sequential
Phase 4
Operator May Take Over
At any point, a human can pause AI and take control of the conversation. They see full context, suggested next actions, and score breakdown. AI stops sending replies until the operator resumes.
human override
Phase 5
Intelligent Routing
Once qualified, Harwick routes the lead to the best agent match: area coverage, property type expertise, price range, availability, and capacity. If no clean match, it escalates to the operator queue.
explainable
Phase 6
Beautiful Handoff
Lead + full conversation history + qualification summary + next action → Follow Up Boss. Agent wakes up to a lead they're ready to close, not a support ticket they have to re-qualify.
context preserved

Team Roles

Who does what

Agent
Receives qualified leads ready to close. Can pause AI, resume AI, or sync to FUB with full context.
  • View their assigned leads queue
  • Control live conversations (pause/resume/takeover)
  • Set their own routing profile (area, type, price range)
  • See showing requests and callbacks
Operator / ISA
Triages the shared work queue. Approves AI replies, escalates edge cases, assigns leads when needed.
  • Review AI-suggested replies before sending
  • Manually take over conversations
  • Reassign leads between agents
  • Manage unrouted / high-touch leads
Team Lead
Manages routing rules, team capacity, and operator queue. Owns agent assignment and oversight.
  • Configure routing rules and priorities
  • View team performance and lead metrics
  • Override assignments and escalations
  • Manage member profiles and capacity
Owner
Owns the workspace and channel integrations. Receives unrouted high-touch leads and can control anything.
  • Connect Instagram, Facebook, Retell voice
  • Set billing and plan features
  • Access full audit logs
  • Override any assignment or routing rule
Design Principles

How Harwick thinks

Automation that pauses for humans

AI handles routine qualification. Humans handle exceptions, edge cases, and high-touch decisions. The system always tells you why it paused.

Context preserved everywhere

Every action—from first reply to final sync—carries full context. No re-qualification. No lost history. No "who was this person again?"

Routing is explainable

Why did this lead go to this agent? Show the work. Score breakdown. Match criteria. Operators can always see and override the reasoning.

One source of truth

Instagram DMs, comments, SMS, voice calls—they're all one lead with one conversation history. No channel silos. No context jumps.
