What is Harwick AI?

Harwick AI is a complete real estate operations platform that handles incoming leads from Instagram, Facebook, and other social channels. It automatically qualifies them, answers questions naturally, routes them to the right agents, and keeps everything in sync with Follow Up Boss and your CRM.

Think of it as a tireless receptionist who:

  • Listens 24/7 to comments and DMs on your social posts
  • Responds naturally with AI that sounds like a real person
  • Qualifies leads by asking smart questions in a conversational way
  • Routes automatically to the best agent based on lead type and availability
  • Syncs with your CRM so agents see full context immediately
  • Lets humans take over anytime — pause the AI, jump in, resume when ready
Bottom line: You get more qualified leads, faster response times, and agents who have context before they even say hello.

Common Questions

Does Harwick AI sound like a robot?

No. It's trained to sound conversational, warm, and human. We spend a lot of time on this because leads won't engage with a bot. Harwick AI passes the "does this sound real?" test in most conversations.

What if I want to jump in and respond personally?

You can pause Harwick AI anytime and take over. The full conversation history is there. You pick up where Harwick AI left off. When you're done, you can resume AI or keep the lead in manual mode.

Will it work for my specific market/niche?

Harwick AI adapts to your market. Whether you're in rural Tennessee or downtown Manhattan, luxury or first-time buyer, investment or primary residence—we configure it for what you do.

What if a lead is outside my service area?

You tell Harwick AI your service areas upfront. If someone asks about a property outside your area, Harwick AI can politely decline or refer them. You control the response.

Can I use this with my existing CRM?

Yes. Harwick AI integrates with Follow Up Boss, and can be configured for others. If it's not built in yet, we can integrate it with your workflow quickly.

What about privacy? Is this secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted. Lead info never goes to a third party. Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and real estate regulations is built in. You own your data.
